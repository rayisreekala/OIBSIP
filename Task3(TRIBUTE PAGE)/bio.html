MOTHER TERESA

ROMAN CATHOLIC NUN

Mother Teresa
The Nobel Peace Prize 1979

Born: 26 August 1910, Uskup, Ottoman Empire (now Skopje, North Macedonia)

Died: 5 September 1997, Calcutta, India

Residence at the time of the award: India

Role: Leader of Missionaries of Charity, Calcutta

Prize motivation: “for her work for bringing help to suffering humanity”

Prize share: 1/1
